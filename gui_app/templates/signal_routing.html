📐 Early Trailing Stop

Moves stop to breakeven at activation %, then locks profit in step increments. Mutually exclusive with Legacy Trailing above.

Activation: % gain
Step Size: % increments

Example: At 5% activation, SL moves to breakeven. At 8% (+3% step), SL locks +3%. At 11%, SL locks +6%.
