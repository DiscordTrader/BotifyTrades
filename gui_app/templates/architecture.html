Risk Architecture

Multi-Layer Risk Engine

Every position is protected by configurable risk brackets with automatic escalation and broker-native order protection

🎯 Progressive Profit Targets

  • Up to 4 independent PT tiers per position
  • Configurable % allocation per tier
  • Dynamic SL raises after each tier hit
  • Remaining runner rides with trailing stop

🛡️ Stop Loss Protection

  • Fixed, percentage, or ATR-based stops
  • Dynamic escalation profiles (standard/aggressive/conservative)
  • Break-even auto-move after PT1 hit
  • OCO bracket orders on Schwab, Alpaca, IBKR

📐 Position Sizing

  • Fixed dollar, fixed shares, or % of portfolio
  • Max position limits per channel and global
  • Duplicate entry prevention
  • Per-broker allocation control

🔄 Bracket Fill Bridge

  • Layer 1: Runtime fill detection via order polling
  • Layer 2: Enriched API with fill-leg attribution
  • Layer 3: Sync attribution with price fallback
  • Layer 4: Restart reconciliation with dynamic SL
